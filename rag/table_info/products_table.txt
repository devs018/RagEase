# Products Table

## Table: products

### Purpose
Catalog of all products sold by the company including pricing, inventory, and supplier information.

### Columns
- **id** (INTEGER PRIMARY KEY): Unique product identifier
- **name** (TEXT NOT NULL): Product name
- **category** (TEXT): Product category (Laptops, Tablets, Smartphones, Audio, Accessories, Monitors)
- **supplier_id** (INTEGER): Foreign key reference to suppliers.id
- **cost_price** (REAL): Cost price from supplier in USD
- **selling_price** (REAL): Retail selling price in USD
- **stock_quantity** (INTEGER): Current inventory level
- **min_stock_level** (INTEGER): Minimum stock threshold for reordering
- **description** (TEXT): Product description
- **created_date** (DATE): When product was added to catalog

### Key Information
- 12 different technology products
- 6 product categories covering major tech segments
- Pricing ranges from $95 to $2,800
- Inventory management with min stock levels
- Products from multiple suppliers
- Mix of premium and standard products

### Sample Data Count
12 products across 6 categories with complete pricing and inventory data.
