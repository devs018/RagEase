# Customers Table

## Table: customers

### Purpose
Information about customers who purchase products from the company.

### Columns
- **id** (INTEGER PRIMARY KEY): Unique customer identifier
- **name** (TEXT NOT NULL): Customer name (company or individual)
- **email** (TEXT UNIQUE): Customer email address
- **phone** (TEXT): Contact phone number
- **address** (TEXT): Customer address
- **city** (TEXT): Customer city
- **country** (TEXT): Customer country
- **customer_type** (TEXT): Type of customer (Enterprise, SMB, Individual, Non-Profit)
- **credit_limit** (REAL): Maximum credit limit in USD
- **registration_date** (DATE): When customer account was created
- **last_purchase_date** (DATE): Most recent purchase date

### Key Information
- 8 customers with diverse profiles
- Mix of enterprise, small business, and individual customers
- Credit limits range from $5,000 to $100,000
- Customers located across different US cities
- Active customer base with recent purchases
- Different customer types for market segmentation

### Sample Data Count
8 customers representing different market segments and sizes.
