# Employees Table

## Table: employees

### Purpose
Comprehensive employee information including personal details, job information, and organizational hierarchy.

### Columns
- **id** (INTEGER PRIMARY KEY): Unique employee identifier
- **name** (TEXT NOT NULL): Full employee name
- **email** (TEXT UNIQUE): Company email address
- **department_id** (INTEGER): Foreign key reference to departments.id
- **position** (TEXT): Job title/position (Senior Developer, Marketing Manager, Sales Representative, etc.)
- **salary** (REAL): Annual salary in USD ranging from $45,000 to $95,000
- **hire_date** (DATE): Employee start date
- **age** (INTEGER): Employee age (24-36 years)
- **manager_id** (INTEGER): Foreign key reference to employees.id for direct manager
- **phone** (TEXT): Contact phone number
- **address** (TEXT): Home address

### Key Information
- 12 total employees across 7 departments
- Hierarchical structure with managers and direct reports
- Salary varies by position and department
- Mix of senior and junior positions
- Complete contact information for all employees
