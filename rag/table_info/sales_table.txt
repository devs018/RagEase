# Sales Table

## Table: sales

### Purpose
Individual sales transaction records for commission tracking and sales performance analysis.

### Columns
- **id** (INTEGER PRIMARY KEY): Unique sale identifier
- **employee_id** (INTEGER): Foreign key reference to employees.id (sales representative)
- **customer_id** (INTEGER): Foreign key reference to customers.id
- **product_id** (INTEGER): Foreign key reference to products.id
- **sale_date** (DATE): Date of sale transaction
- **quantity** (INTEGER): Number of units sold
- **unit_price** (REAL): Price per unit in USD
- **total_amount** (REAL): Total sale amount
- **commission** (REAL): Commission earned by sales rep (5% of total_amount)
- **region** (TEXT): Sales region (North, South, East, West, Central)
- **sale_type** (TEXT): Type of sale (Direct, Online, Partner, Referral)

### Key Information
- 100 individual sales transactions
- Commission structure for sales performance tracking
- Regional sales distribution
- Multiple sale types for channel analysis
- Sales made by dedicated sales employees
- Historical sales data spanning one year

100 sales records with complete transaction details and commission calculations.
