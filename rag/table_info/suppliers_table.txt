# Suppliers Table

## Table: suppliers

### Purpose
Information about external suppliers providing products to the company.

### Columns
- **id** (INTEGER PRIMARY KEY): Unique supplier identifier
- **name** (TEXT NOT NULL): Supplier company name
- **contact_person** (TEXT): Primary contact person name
- **email** (TEXT): Contact email address
- **phone** (TEXT): Contact phone number
- **address** (TEXT): Supplier address
- **city** (TEXT): Supplier city
- **country** (TEXT): Supplier country (USA, UK, Germany)
- **rating** (REAL): Supplier performance rating (3.9 to 4.7 scale)
- **contract_start_date** (DATE): When partnership began

### Key Information
- 5 international suppliers
- Mix of technology and electronics suppliers
- Performance ratings to track supplier quality
- Global supplier base including USA, UK, and Germany
- Long-term partnerships established 2019-2021

### Sample Data Count
5 suppliers providing various technology products.
