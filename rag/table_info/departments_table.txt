# Departments Table

## Table: departments

### Purpose
Stores information about company departments including budget, location, and management structure.

### Columns
- **id** (INTEGER PRIMARY KEY): Unique department identifier
- **name** (TEXT NOT NULL UNIQUE): Department name (Engineering, Marketing, Sales, HR, Finance, Operations, Customer Service)
- **manager_id** (INTEGER): Foreign key reference to employees.id for department manager
- **budget** (REAL): Annual department budget in USD
- **location** (TEXT): Physical location of department (San Francisco, New York, Chicago, Austin, Boston, Seattle, Denver)
- **created_date** (DATE): Date when department was established

### Key Information
- Each department has a unique name
- Budget ranges from $200,000 to $500,000
- Manager is an employee from the same or different department
- Locations are distributed across major US cities

### Sample Data Count
7 departments total covering all major business functions.
