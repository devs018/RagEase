# Products - Suppliers Join

## Join Relationship
**products.supplier_id = suppliers.id**

## Join Type
Many-to-One (Many products can come from one supplier)

## Join Purpose
Link products to their suppliers for supply chain analysis and vendor management.

## Common Join Queries
```sql
-- Products with supplier information
SELECT p.name as product, p.category, p.selling_price, s.name as supplier, s.country, s.rating
FROM products p
JOIN suppliers s ON p.supplier_id = s.id

-- Supplier product catalog and performance
SELECT s.name as supplier, COUNT(p.id) as product_count, AVG(p.selling_price) as avg_product_price, s.rating
FROM suppliers s
LEFT JOIN products p ON s.id = p.supplier_id
GROUP BY s.id, s.name

-- Cost analysis by supplier
SELECT s.name as supplier, SUM(p.cost_price * p.stock_quantity) as total_inventory_cost
FROM suppliers s
JOIN products p ON s.id = p.supplier_id
GROUP BY s.id, s.name
```

## Key Insights Available
- Supplier diversity by product category
- Cost analysis by supplier
- Supplier performance vs product pricing
- Geographic supplier distribution
- Inventory value by supplier
