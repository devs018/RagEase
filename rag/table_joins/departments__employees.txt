# Departments - Employees Join

## Join Relationship
**departments.id = employees.department_id**

## Join Type
One-to-Many (One department can have many employees)

## Join Purpose
Link employees to their respective departments for organizational structure analysis.

## Common Join Queries
```sql
-- Get employees with department information
SELECT e.name, e.position, e.salary, d.name as department_name, d.location, d.budget
FROM employees e
JOIN departments d ON e.department_id = d.id

-- Department headcount and average salary
SELECT d.name as department, COUNT(e.id) as employee_count, AVG(e.salary) as avg_salary
FROM departments d
LEFT JOIN employees e ON d.id = e.department_id
GROUP BY d.id, d.name

-- Department managers
SELECT d.name as department, mgr.name as manager_name, mgr.salary as manager_salary
FROM departments d
JOIN employees mgr ON d.manager_id = mgr.id
```

## Key Insights Available
- Department workforce distribution
- Salary analysis by department
- Management hierarchy
- Department budget vs employee costs
- Geographic employee distribution
